The Farm
Situated on the outskirts of the town of Seia, with views across the mountains of the Serra da Estrela, this beautifully restored farmstead has a decidedly homely feel, that will make you not want to leave.
Farmed by the Ferrão family for many generations, the Quinta produces a wide variety of homegrown produce whilst our vineyard provides grapes for the local wine coop.
In the rambling, shady grounds you'll find snoozing cats and our friendly farm dog "Faisca".
Choose between a room in the main house or a more private space in one of our two Outhouses.
Main House
Completely renovated in 2016, the main house contains 6 bedrooms, all of which come equipped with TV and ensuite bathroom.
A large dining room boasting an open fire and modern kitchen facilities provides ample space for eating and socialising.
Oh, and a brand new pool to cool down in during those long hot summer days.

Region
In both summer and winter this is an ideal base from which to discover the Serra da Estrela, Portugal's highest mountain range.
With its bizarrely shaped crags and gorges, mountain streams and lakes, beautiful forests and magnificent views, the region is one of Portugal’s most outstanding scenic attractions.
The park is excellent for trekking, horse-riding, or mountain biking with some 375km of marked trails of varying degrees of difficulty.
In cold weather, Serra da Estrela is the only place in Portugal where you can ski, offering several lift served pistes.
To recharge your batteries, you can taste the most famous product in the region - the Serra da Estrela cheese. With its buttery texture, it goes ideally with the traditional local bread.
